Our Story

Inspired by the royal kitchens of the Nawabs, The Grand Awadh brings centuries-old recipes to the modern diner. Our journey began with a single mission: to preserve the delicate art of 'Dum' cooking.


Under the guidance of our Head Chef, whose lineage traces back to the imperial khansamas, every dish is a labor of love, balanced with secret spice blends and slow-cooked to perfection.
